About This Course

This course, "Computer Course: Beginner to Pro," is designed for anyone who wants to learn the essential skills needed to use a computer confidently. Whether you are a student, job seeker, teacher, or business owner, this course will guide you step by step through the basics of computers, the internet, Microsoft Office, and digital communication tools. It combines theory with practical exercises to help you build real-world computer skills. By the end of the course, you will be comfortable using a computer for everyday tasks, professional work, and personal productivity.

What You Will Learn

In this course, you will learn a wide range of practical skills that form the foundation of digital literacy. These include how to operate a computer, understand hardware and software, manage files and folders, and browse the internet securely. You will also learn how to create documents using Microsoft Word, organize data using Excel, and design presentations with PowerPoint. Additionally, you will gain essential knowledge about email communication and staying safe online. All lessons are beginner-friendly, clearly explained, and include hands-on activities to help you apply what you learn immediately.

Prerequisites

This course is designed for complete beginners, so no prior computer knowledge is required. All you need is a willingness to learn and access to a smartphone, tablet, or computer. If you have a basic understanding of reading and writing in English or Swahili, you will be able to follow the lessons easily.
